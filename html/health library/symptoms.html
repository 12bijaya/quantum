Common Cold

The symptoms of the common cold include:

  • Runny or stuffy nose
  • Sore throat
  • Cough
  • Sneezing
  • Low-grade fever
  • Body aches
  • Headache

Influenza (Flu)

The symptoms of influenza (flu) may include:

  • Fever or feeling feverish/chills
  • Cough
  • Sore throat
  • Runny or stuffy nose
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headaches
  • Fatigue (tiredness)
  • Vomiting and diarrhea (more common in children than adults)

Allergies

The symptoms of allergies may include:

  • Sneezing
  • Runny or stuffy nose
  • Watery or itchy eyes
  • Itchy throat, nose, or ears
  • Swelling or dark circles under the eyes
  • Coughing
  • Wheezing
  • Rash or hives
  • Fatigue

Asthma

The symptoms of asthma may include:

  • Shortness of breath
  • Chest tightness or pain
  • Coughing (especially at night or early morning)
  • Wheezing

Diabetes (Type 1 and Type 2)

The symptoms of diabetes may include:

  • Increased thirst
  • Frequent urination
  • Extreme hunger
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fatigue
  • Blurred vision
  • Slow-healing sores
  • Frequent infections

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

The symptoms of hypertension may include:

  • Headaches
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness
  • Chest pain
  • Blurred or double vision
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Palpitations
  • Nosebleeds
